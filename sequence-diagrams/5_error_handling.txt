title Handling STT or AI Processing Failures

User->>STT Provider: Speak input
STT Provider->X Buffer Manager: STT fails (no transcription)
STT Provider->>Error Handler: Notify of STT failure
Error Handler->>User: "I couldn't understand, can you repeat that?"

User->>STT Provider: Speak input again
STT Provider->>Buffer Manager: Transcribe text in 1s chunks
Buffer Manager->X LLM Provider: AI model fails to generate response
LLM Provider->>Error Handler: Notify of AI failure
Error Handler->>User: "I couldn't process your request, try again."
